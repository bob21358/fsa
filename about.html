- About the FSA -

The Finch Society of Australia Inc. was formed with the simple aim of bringing together people who are dedicated to the hobby and business of the keeping and breeding of finches.

The Society, of more than 50 years standing includes the Woolongong, Hawkesbury and Hunter Valley branches.

Monthly meetings consist of the usual society business followed by either an address from guest speaker or a talk from local finch breeders or video or slide night or demonstration or just a members forum whereby we try to sort out new members problems and those of members too!! Worming methods, feeding, breeding and designing aviaries are but a few of the more popular topics.
